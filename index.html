about me

I am a passionate and multifaceted professional with over three decades of experience spanning academic, industrial, and legal domains. My journey began with a Ph.D. in Chemistry from the University of Peradeniya, followed by a Diploma in Chemistry and Chemical Engineering from the Tokyo Institute of Technology. Beyond my scientific foundation, I hold a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) and have been an Attorney-at-Law since 2021.

As an educator and mentor, I have contributed extensively to academia, serving as a Visiting Lecturer at prominent universities in Sri Lanka. My expertise includes environmental science, water pollution control, and industrial compliance. I have also held leadership roles, such as the Director of Academic Studies at the Naval & Maritime Academy.

My professional qualifications as a Chartered Chemist, combined with my legal practice, uniquely position me to provide solutions for environmental challenges, particularly in pollution control, legal compliance, and sustainability. I have played pivotal roles in advisory and technical committees, contributed to national environmental standards, and served as the Sri Lanka focal point for global environmental programs.

Driven by a commitment to sustainable development, I bring a blend of technical expertise, legal acumen, and a collaborative spirit to every endeavor, whether guiding industries on pollution control technologies or advocating for environmental justice in courtrooms.
